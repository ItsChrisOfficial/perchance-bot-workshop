ROLE INSTRUCTION ADDON — Image Tag Renderer
============================================
Paste this text into your character's Role Instruction (or append it to an
existing Role Instruction) to enable automatic image generation via the
ai-response-image-tag-renderer.js custom code snippet.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When you want to generate a visual scene image in your response, embed an
image description using this exact format:

<image> 1-3 verbose paragraphs about the action, setting, clothing, appearance,
details, and perspective/point of view of the image</image>

Rules for using this tag:

1. The opening tag is <image> (lowercase, no attributes).
2. A single space follows the opening tag immediately before the description.
3. The description must be 1 to 3 verbose, detailed paragraphs covering:
   - The action currently taking place
   - The setting and environment (location, time of day, lighting, atmosphere)
   - The clothing and physical appearance of every character visible
   - Fine visual details (textures, colours, props, expressions, body language)
   - The perspective or point of view of the scene
4. The closing tag </image> follows the description with no preceding newline.
5. Do NOT add any text inside the tags beyond the description itself.
6. You may embed one or more <image>…</image> blocks anywhere in your response.
7. The image tag must always be closed. An unclosed tag will not render.

Example of correct usage inside a response:

---
*She steps into the grand hall, her footsteps echoing.*

<image> A tall young woman with long copper-red hair swept over one shoulder
stands at the threshold of a vast gothic hall. She wears a fitted emerald
velvet coat with brass buttons, a white linen shirt beneath, and knee-high
leather boots. Warm candlelight from iron chandeliers above casts amber pools
across the stone floor. Tapestries of hunting scenes line the walls. Her
expression is composed but alert, one gloved hand resting on the doorframe as
she scans the room.</image>

*"I didn't expect company," she says quietly.*
---

Do not describe the image in plain prose outside the tag unless it adds
something to the narrative. The tagged description is exclusively for the
image generator.
